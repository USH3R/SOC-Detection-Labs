# SOC Blue Team — TryHackMe

## Summary
Completed the SOC Role / Blue Team room on TryHackMe. Learned how SOC teams operate to defend organizations, respond to alerts, and handle security incidents.

## Key Takeaways
- Blue Team focuses on defensive security and monitoring for threats  
- L1 analysts triage alerts, L2 analysts investigate advanced attacks  
- Engineers maintain and configure security tools for effective detection  
- Managers oversee the SOC and coordinate with other teams  
- Cyber Incident Response Team (CIRT) handles urgent or critical incidents
